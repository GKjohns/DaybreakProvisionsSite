How to add this signature to your email client:

Gmail:

  1. Click "Copy Signature to Clipboard" above

  4. Click "Create new" and name your signature

Apple Mail:

  1. Click "Copy Signature to Clipboard" above
  2. Open Mail ‚Üí Preferences ‚Üí Signatures
  3. Click the "+" button to create a new signature
  4. Paste (Cmd+V) the signature
  5. Uncheck "Always match my default message font"
  6. Drag the signature to your email account

Outlook:

  1. Click "Copy Signature to Clipboard" above
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí View all Outlook settings
  3. Select Mail ‚Üí Compose and reply
  4. Under "Email signature", paste the signature
  5. Click "Save"
